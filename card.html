PICTOR I

This PICTOR I armchair looks marvelous in any open space – in fact it's a joy to view not only face-on, but also from behind. Its unusual combination of fabrics and textures achieve a trompe l'œil effect, making you want to sense its appearance in a truly tactile way. The perfect synergy of these textures with the wood-grain taken on the 'cut' line is no mere coincidence – but the result of the cabinetmaker's skilled mastery.
